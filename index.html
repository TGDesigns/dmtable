Notes - Rule updates

Table Talk
Above table talk at a minimum please. Please try and keep all discussions in character if related to story and actions (not if its a rule question). 'Critical Role play' as much as possible. This WILL affect gameplay.

For example, if your talking about whether you're going to attack someone or kill them after - you better whisper and roll stealth cause they may have heard you...

This is in an effort to produce individual character decisions and character development rather than herd mentality.
Long Rests
Long Rests are going to be more difficult. You need to have ample conditions to take a long rest. Otherwise, a short rest will be necessary. This means walls and lodging or guard shifts if in the wilderness with somewhere where you can get 8 hours of sleep.

This is in an effort to bring awareness to surroundings and danger, make your headstrong pushes in to battle more critical, as well as add more critical decision making and bring short rests value up.
Travel Time
Travel is going to be more vital. Depending on how you are going to get somewhere we will not just skip over weeks of travel. We may montage long travel periods but most travel will be in game ot an extent.

This is in an effort to add more random encounters have more interesting and unique situations with your surroundings happen. Lets learn more about the Sword Coast together!
Birthday Crit
When its your birthday you have one critical roll to use within the next two sessions.

Notes - Setting Details

This Campaign takes place in the forgotten realms in Fae'run on the Sword Coast. For those of you from my first campaign at Phandelver, that was a small taste of the world but just a small glimpse.

For those familiar with the Sword Coast, all the lore, factions, history, cities and distance will be applicable here when it matters and it will matter.

We are in the same setting as our previous campaign but roughly 100 years in the future. Your former characters were not much heard of as they dissapeared upon exploring wave echo cave and never returned with the exception of stories past down from a few adventurers long before this time. About 400 years ago, A Rudolph Van Richten and his protege, Ezmerelda, wrote a "guide to Ravenloft" which mentioned individuals matching your missing adventurers but have not been heard from again.

Prologue

As the sun sets over the Sword Coast, casting a warm glow on the bustling city of Waterdeep, a diverse group of adventurers find themselves drawn together by a shared destiny. Each of them carries a weighty past, their stories interwoven in the tapestry of this vast and vibrant land.

The Sword Coast, a stretch of land that hugs the western shoreline of Faerûn, is a region teeming with life, diversity, and the echoes of countless tales. It is a place where ancient civilizations have risen and fallen, leaving behind ruins and legends for the intrepid to discover. Cities like Waterdeep, Neverwinter, and Baldur's Gate serve as beacons of civilization amidst the untamed wilderness that surrounds them.

The adventurers, from various walks of life, have found themselves converging upon Waterdeep, a city of grandeur and intrigue. Known as the City of Splendors, it is a melting pot of different races, cultures, and ambitions. Within its walls, the Lords of Waterdeep and its renowned factions hold sway over the affairs of the region.

These factions, each with its own agenda and methods, vie for power and influence in the face of the ever-changing landscape. From the noble Order of the Gauntlet, sworn to protect the innocent and uphold justice, to the enigmatic Harpers, dedicated to preserving the balance of power and knowledge, the adventurers have been drawn to one or more of these organizations.

The Sword Coast is a tapestry of stories waiting to be unraveled. It is a land where heroes rise, and legends are born. Within its borders, dragons soar through the skies, their ancient wisdom and power awe-inspiring yet fearsome. Giants, towering beings of immense strength, have begun to show their faces once more, their return after ages shrouded in mystery and foreboding.

The adventurers, each with their own unique backgrounds and aspirations, yearn for a purpose greater than themselves. They seek answers to the questions that linger in their hearts and minds, the whispers of destiny guiding their steps. They are not alone in their quest, for others have joined their cause, united by a shared yearning for adventure and the hope of making a difference.

As they set out from Waterdeep, the adventurers will traverse the length and breadth of the Sword Coast. They will pass through bustling trade routes, delve into ancient ruins steeped in history, and navigate treacherous wilderness teeming with hidden dangers. Along their journey, they will encounter a rich tapestry of races, from the sturdy Dwarves of the Spine of the World to the ethereal Elves of the High Forest.

Legends and tales of forgotten realms will be their companions, guiding them through the trials and tribulations that lie ahead. They will witness the machinations of powerful entities, both mortal and divine, whose influence weaves through the very fabric of the realms. Gods like Tyr the Just, Lathander the Morninglord, and Mystra the All-Seeing watch over their mortal charges, offering guidance and protection in times of need.

Through their travels, the adventurers will uncover the intricacies of the Sword Coast's political landscape, forging alliances and making enemies as they strive to fulfill their purpose. They will learn of the simmering tensions between factions, the delicate balance of power that shapes the fate of nations, and the ancient prophecies that foretell of great upheavals.

But above all, they will discover the strength that lies within themselves. The Sword Coast is a crucible that will test their mettle, challenging them to rise above their limitations, confront their fears, and embrace their destinies as heroes.

And so, as the moon rises high in the night sky, our heroes take their first steps into the unknown, their hearts filled with hope and determination
